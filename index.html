About Me

My name is Stavros. I live in lovely Thessaloniki but I enjoy travelling around the world. I have graduated from school at 2010 and started my studies in computer science at the Applied Informatics department of University of Macedonia. I was not passionate with computer science as a student but I found love on programming just right after my graduation at 2016. My journey as a developer started one month before my graduation when I started working as a hybrid mobile developer and a web application developer.

If you would ask me what programing means to me I would tell you programing is coffee and music, anger and fun, despair and excitement, dry eyes and total satisfaction. In my free time you will find me scrolling around stackoverflow, working on my own or freelance projects or studying about new trends and tools around the web world.

Project Details

The Butterflies of Greece is the digital edition of the well-known relative book of Mr. Lazaros Pamperis. Project was developed with Ionic framework and is served on all popular platforms. The application consists of two versions. The first presents a huge collection of rare beautiful species. The latter also includes the book itself on digital form.
